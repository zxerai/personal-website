## Why this site

Friends in tech often ask me: "What are you working on lately?" The answer is always scattered — building a RAG system, writing an agent framework, debugging a prompt. All of this lives in GitHub commits and Twitter threads, with no central home.

So I decided to build this site, gathering my projects, thoughts, and work in one place.

## What you'll find here

- **Projects**: Things I've built with code, focused on AI/LLM applications
- **Blog**: Thoughts on AI engineering and product engineering
- **Resume**: My professional experience and skills
- **About**: Who I am, what I care about

## Writing style

I tend to write directly, no beating around the bush. If a tool is bad, I'll say so. If an idea isn't mature, I'll mark it as such. I don't want this to be a "marketing site" — it should be an honest record.
